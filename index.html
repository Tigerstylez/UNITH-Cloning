Theory & Best Practices

Quality matters

The fidelity of your clone depends heavily on the source video quality. Use a sharp, well-lit, front-facing shot with a neutral background and minimal noise.

For more tips on recording the best possible video, download our guide:
Open Recording Guide (PDF)

Recording: Stable front-facing camera, even lighting, neutral background. Avoid harsh shadows and noise.

Input: Video in MP4/H.264, 10s looking at the camera, 16:9 HD (1280x720p) at 25fps or 1080p.

Process: Obtain your Bearer Token → Generate → Validate result → Iterate.

Ethics & Legal: Always obtain and store explicit consent from the individual whose image is being cloned.

Obtain your Bearer Token

Get your Secret Key:

  1. Open UNITH interface: Click your name in the top-right corner.
  2. Manage Account: Select "Manage Account" from the dropdown.
  3. Generate secret key: Click "Generate Secret Key". Copy the key immediately, you will not be able to view it again.
  4. Store securely: Save the Secret Key in a secrets manager or environment variable (do not embed it in client-side code or commit it to source control).

Get your Bearer Token:

  1. Open Your Terminal: Launch your terminal or command line interface on your machine.
  2. Run the following command: Use your Email and Secret Key. 
    curl -X 'POST' \
    'https://platform-api.unith.ai/auth/token' \
    -H 'accept: application/json' \
    -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
    -d '{
    "email": "YOUR_EMAIL",
    "secretKey": "YOUR_UNITH_SECRET_KEY"
    }'
  3. This will return Your Bearer Token (valid for 7 days)

What’s the difference between Default mode and Two Loops?

Default mode focuses on speed and standard-quality results using a single pass over your video. It creates only one idle animation loop.
For greater expressiveness, we’ve introduced Two Loops. it defines two states: one for idle (when silent), and another for talking.
This gives your head visuals more dynamism and realism. You upload one video as usual, but define the exact transition point between idle and talking using the cut timestamp parameter.

Can I clone an animal?

No. Our model is trained to recognize human faces. You can try, but the output will likely not be accurate or usable for animal faces.

Is there a minimum quality required?

Yes. If the input doesn’t meet the minimum quality, facial features may not be detected correctly and the process can fail. Aim for:

  • Resolution: 720p or 1080p
  • Duration: 10 seconds maximum, looking at the camera
  • Lighting: even, front-facing light; avoid harsh shadows
  • Background: clean/neutral; minimal noise and movement
  • Framing: stable, head centered, 16:9 aspect ratio

What’s most important to make it work well?

  • Quality: sharp focus and consistent lighting.
  • Size: sufficient resolution (720p+).
  • Movement: avoid sudden or fast head movements.
